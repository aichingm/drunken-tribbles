Drunken-Tribbles was written and is maintained by Mario Aichinger (aichingm)

Drunken-Tribbles is a library for creating images filled with triangles. The triangles can be configured to have fixed sizes or they can have some fuzziness. The library supports diffrent types of methods of colorizing the triangles.
